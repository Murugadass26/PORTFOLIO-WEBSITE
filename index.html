Underwater ROV for HRTs

Underwater Rov

ROVs transform headrace tunnel inspections by avoiding draining, unlike conventional methods that incur expensive downtime. They offer real-time inspection with high-definition cameras and sonar, identifying cracks,sedimentation, and structural flaws while the plant is in operation.

Their capability to move through submerged tunnels allows for quicker, safer, and more effective inspections without exposing workers to dangerous underwater conditions. In contrast to manual inspections,which are labor-intensive and time-consuming, ROVs provide accurate, on-invasive inspections with minimal disruption.
